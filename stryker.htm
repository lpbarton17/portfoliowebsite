Biomechanical Intern

I completed my second co-op at Stryker, Trauma and Extremeties in Kiel, Germany. From July-December 2017, I worked as a biomechancial intern. I gained experience in the testing of medical devices and creation of CAD models in Creo ProEngineer.

CAD

I worked on the distal femur foam model test setup shown to the right. The setup applied load to a nail assembly to test the durability and strength of a nail and screws. I used ProE to alter the design of the steel components and incorporate the yellow foam mold into the setup.

Foam Molding

I gained experience in foam molding and I also designed a mold form to create a distal femur mold.
The two halves of the mold form allowed reproducibility and easy removal of the foamed piece for accurate testing.
In addition to the mold form, a screw template was needed to ensure exact screw placement in each foamed piece. I created the design to be easily used by one person.
